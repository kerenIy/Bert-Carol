Bert & Carol

Origin

Bert & Carol was founded in Port Harcourt, Nigeria in 2022. It was created when it became clear that the education concerning skin care in Nigeria and the likes of it was faulty and misguiding also the reach for quality skin care products produced in the country was almost obselete.

Who are we?

Our Mission

Our mission as a brand is to make skin care and skin education more transparent and accessible while giving our customers complete deiscretion. We also aim to create only the best and most effiecient product to suit our customers needs.

Our Vision

Our vision is to reach the needs of many Nigerians and Africans at large.
